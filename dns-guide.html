Connecting Your New Website

A simple guide for Ajmer's Indian Restaurant

Prepared by Zac Nowytarger
Questions? Call 0401 102 066
To connect your new website, we need to change one setting in your Crazy Domains account called "nameservers." This points your domain to my hosting system where the new site lives. That's all you need to do — I'll take care of everything else, including making sure your email keeps working.

Will this affect my email?

Before we make any changes, I'll document all your current email settings and make sure they're set up on the new system first. Your email will keep working as normal — I'll handle that side of things so you don't have to worry about it. I won't ask you to make the change until everything is ready.

What you need to do in Crazy Domains
1

Log into Crazy Domains

Go to crazydomains.com.au and sign in with your account. If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" to reset it.

2

Find your domain

Click "Domains" in the top menu, then "Manage" from the left menu. Click on ajmers.com.au.

3

Find the Nameservers setting

Scroll down to the "Nameservers" section. You'll see it currently shows the old values below. Click "Modify" next to Nameservers.

Current NS 1 alec.ns.cloudflare.com
Current NS 2 cruz.ns.cloudflare.com
4

Replace with the new nameservers

Delete the old ones and type in exactly:

New NS 1 dave.ns.cloudflare.com
New NS 2 shubhi.ns.cloudflare.com
5

Save

Click "Update". Crazy Domains may ask to confirm — click Yes.

How long does it take? The change usually kicks in within a few hours, but can take up to 24 hours. During this time your old site may still show for some visitors — this is normal and nothing will break. I will have already set up your email records on the new system before you make this change, so your email will continue working throughout.

That's all you need to do — I'll handle everything else.

Before you make this change, I'll contact you to confirm that your email settings have been transferred and everything is ready. Once you've saved the new nameservers, let me know and I'll activate the new site, set up the security certificate, and make sure everything works. If you get stuck, call me on 0401 102 066.
