Our Story

Balgowlah's Very First Indian Restaurant

Ajmer's Indian Restaurant is a family-owned Indian restaurant on Sydney's Northern Beaches, serving authentic curries, tandoori, and naan from two locations in Balgowlah and Belrose since 1990.

In 1990, when the Northern Beaches had never tasted a proper tandoori, Manjit and Daljit Singh Saini opened a small Indian restaurant on Sydney Road, Balgowlah. Nobody had tried it before. It was a risk — and it changed the neighbourhood forever.

Thirty-six years later, Chef Daljit is still in the kitchen. Not supervising — cooking. The same hands that lit the tandoor on opening night still stretch every naan, still stir every curry, still taste every sauce before it leaves the pass. The butter chicken recipe hasn't changed. The spice blends are still ground in-house. The only thing different is how far people drive to eat it.

Regulars drive from Mosman for the butter chicken. Couples who had their first date here now book the same table with their kids. We've been called "one of Sydney's most romantic dinner destinations" — but to us, romance is just what happens when the food is made with that much care.

We're not a chain. We're not a trend. We're a family, and we'd love to welcome you to our table.

Before You Visit

Got a question? We've probably answered it below. If not, give us a ring — we'd love to chat.

We have two beautiful locations on Sydney's Northern Beaches. Our original restaurant has been at Shop 7, 373 Sydney Rd, Balgowlah since 1990. Our second home is at Shop 32, 56-58 Glen St, Glenrose Village Shopping Centre, Belrose. Both are easy to find with plenty of free parking.

Balgowlah: Monday to Thursday 5pm–10pm, Friday & Saturday 5pm–10:30pm, Sunday 5pm–10pm.
Belrose: Monday to Friday 11:30am–9pm, Saturday 1:30pm–9pm, Sunday 4:30pm–9pm.

Absolutely! Both our Balgowlah and Belrose restaurants offer free parking right at the door. No meters, no stress — just pull up and come on in.

Yes! We're BYO for wine and beer, so feel free to bring your favourite bottle. We're also fully licensed, so you can order from our drinks menu if you prefer. Either way, you're sorted.

We certainly do. We offer a wonderful range of vegetarian dishes — Malai Kofta, Aloo Gobhi, Vegetable Kadhai, Palak Paneer, and many more. Several dishes on our menu are naturally gluten-free and clearly marked. Just let us know about any dietary needs and we'll take care of you.

Yes, we deliver through Uber Eats and Menulog from both locations. Of course, you can also order takeaway directly by calling us — and we'd love to see you pick it up in person!

The easiest way is to give us a call. For Balgowlah, ring (02) 9948 5297. For Belrose, call (02) 9451 8875. We're always happy to hear from you and will do our best to find you the perfect table.

We opened our doors in 1990 — that's 36 years of serving the Northern Beaches! We were Balgowlah's very first Indian restaurant, and we're incredibly proud of the community we've become a part of. Some of our regulars have been dining with us since the very beginning.

That's like asking us to pick a favourite child! But honestly, the Butter Chicken is the one that built our reputation — people have been ordering it since 1990, and the recipe hasn't changed a day. The Lamb Korma, Onion Bhaji, and Kerala Prawns are close behind. But start with the butter chicken. Trust us.

Very much so! We love welcoming families. We have plenty of mild options for the little ones, and our staff are always happy to accommodate children. Many of our guests started coming as kids themselves — and now they bring their own children. That's the kind of restaurant we always wanted to be.

Our Balgowlah restaurant is just about 5 minutes by car from Manly — it's a very easy drive. We're a popular spot for Manly locals looking for a fantastic Indian meal without the Corso crowds.

We do indeed! Takeaway is available from both our Balgowlah and Belrose restaurants. Just call ahead and your order will be ready when you arrive. We also deliver through Uber Eats and Menulog if you'd rather stay on the couch.

Our Belrose location serves lunch Monday to Friday, opening at 11:30am — perfect for a midday curry! Our Balgowlah restaurant is dinner-only, opening at 5pm each evening. So if you're craving a lunchtime feast, head to Belrose.
