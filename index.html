Our Story

Balgowlah's Very First Indian Restaurant

In 1990, when the Northern Beaches had never tasted a proper tandoori, Manjit and Daljit Singh Saini opened the doors of Ajmer's Indian Restaurant on Sydney Road, Balgowlah. It was a bold move — and it changed the neighbourhood forever.

Thirty-six years later, those same hands are still in the kitchen. Chef Daljit, with over 30 years of culinary mastery, brings the same passion to every dish that he did on opening night. Every curry is slow-simmered with care. Every naan is hand-stretched and charred in the tandoor. Every plate carries three decades of tradition.

Our regulars have been dining with us for generations — parents who came on first dates now bring their children. Described as "one of Sydney's most romantic dinner destinations," we've built something rare: a place where rich, aromatic flavours meet genuine warmth and hospitality.

We're not a chain. We're not a trend. We're a family, and we'd love to welcome you to our table.

We have two beautiful locations on Sydney's Northern Beaches. Our original restaurant has been at Shop 7, 373 Sydney Rd, Balgowlah since 1990. Our second home is at Shop 32, 56-58 Glen St, Glenrose Village Shopping Centre, Belrose. Both are easy to find with plenty of free parking.

Balgowlah: Monday to Thursday 5pm–10pm, Friday & Saturday 5pm–10:30pm, Sunday 5pm–10pm.
Belrose: Monday to Friday 11:30am–9pm, Saturday 1:30pm–9pm, Sunday 4:30pm–9pm.

That's like asking us to pick a favourite child! But if we had to choose, our Butter Chicken is legendary — it's the dish that's kept people coming back for over three decades. The Lamb Korma, Onion Bhaji, and Kerala Prawns Curry are also firm favourites. Honestly, you can't go wrong.

Finding Us on the Northern Beaches

Both locations offer ample free parking and are well-connected by bus routes across the Northern Beaches.
